Our Team

The People Behind Bio Pod

Bio Pod brings together practical product thinking, renewable-energy engineering, app-connected monitoring, and sustainability design to transform everyday organic waste into useful energy.

Our founding team is building compact biogas technology for homes, hostels, campuses, canteens, and institutions that want cleaner fuel, lower waste, and smarter circular-energy infrastructure.
