Our Technology

Next-Generation Smart Biogas Technology

Bio Pod is a compact biogas system designed to convert everyday food waste into clean biogas and nutrient-rich organic slurry. With integrated sensing, safe gas handling, and a connected mobile application, the system helps users manage waste efficiently while monitoring performance in real time.

01 Product Overview

A New Category of Compact Waste-to-Energy Infrastructure

Bio Pod transforms organic waste from homes, hostels, canteens, and campuses into a usable energy source. Instead of sending food waste to landfills, the system digests it inside a controlled chamber and captures the generated biogas for practical use.

The connected mobile application gives users real-time visibility into fill level, gas generation status, safety alerts, and maintenance reminders, making the entire process simple, trackable, and user-friendly.

  • Converts food waste into biogas
  • Produces organic slurry
  • Compact indoor / outdoor install
  • App-based monitoring & alerts
  • Safe gas collection & storage
  • Low-maintenance operation
